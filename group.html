Fist global Service Month

Character Workshop（Press Start Academy）

Studio Hong Kong and Singapore collaborated with press start academy, an education company based in Hong Kong, to organize character workshops for students aged 8-14.In the two-hour course, 20 students gathered at the fist game Hong Kong office to learn how to create a character from scratch. The students worked in groups in classrooms themed by Ionia, frejord, shadow island and piltwalf, using two sets of tables focusing on appearance and background stories. Fist illustrators demonstrate how to give life to their creativity and sketches. After the course, students can take home their new professional hand-painted characters. I believe you can guess that the atmosphere of the studio can be said to be climax.

Shanghai Sign Language (Shanghai Handy Holding Dream Sign Language Cultural Center)

The Shanghai Office cooperated with Shanghai suishoumeng sign language culture center to hold a two-day sign language training camp. Two teachers and an interpreter introduced deaf culture to the staff and carried out basic Shanghai sign language teaching. A total of 48 fist employees participated in the course, and the atmosphere was very warm. Many students also signed up to participate in the service project for deaf children of the cultural center, preparing to participate in the learning and game activities of the center in their spare time.

Winter Clothing Donation (Banho Solidario SAMPA)

Fist employees were invited to donate warm winter clothes to the organization during the global service month. Affected by COVID-19's economy, the number of homeless people in St Paul, Brazil has increased. For every piece of clothing donated, the fist Brazil office promised to donate 150 Brazilian reais to non-profit organizations to help them prepare showers and clean clothes for more homeless people. The final total donation amount is more than 13000 Brazilian reais (about 2500 US dollars), which can help nearly 1300 homeless people in Sao Paulo.
