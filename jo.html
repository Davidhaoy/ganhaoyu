Tencent acquisition

On December 17, 2015, Riot Games, the developer of League of Legends, announced on its official blog that Tencent has acquired 100 percent of Riot Games, making It a wholly owned subsidiary of Tencent.

In 2008, Tencent invested in Riot Games for a 22.34% stake. In November 2011, Tencent spent another $231 million in cash to increase its stake, leaving Tencent with a 92.78% stake after the transaction. At the time, Tencent said it would ensure the independence of Riot Games' operations and management. League of Legends is now the most played MOBA, and Tencent already has a controlling stake in Riot Games
